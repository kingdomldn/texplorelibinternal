Spinner Modules

Spinner modules are one of the more complex modules to both create & execute. The module presents the user with a full screen scene - this has a small area of descriptive text to describe a feature or function. Depending on the module option chosen the user can either freespin the image by swiping quickly or snap to the next scene. When swiping the image is effectively spinning in 360 degrees on a flatplane. This can give the illusion of the car moving through a landscape as shown in the RAV4 8 image spinner.

When creating these experiences the asset production time will need to be taken into account. As this is a large scale circular image. Due to the 360 degree nature of the module the background image needs to maintain a circular style in order to appear smooth.

Variants available:
