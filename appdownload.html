App Download

The Toyota Explore app is available for both iOS & Android platforms. The app works on both tablets and phones. We recommend a minimum of iPhone 5 and iPad air 1st generation when viewing the app on iOS & a Samsung S6, Nexus 5, Nexus 9 or other 1920px X 1080px based Android device. The app is free to download in both stores available content will vary across regions based on what has been produced for that market.

Some markets do not have all content released Europe wide. If you require access or wish to see additional content other teams or cars have used you will need to download the private production release of the app. This app contains different content to that of the public release app and also may have work in progress modules.
