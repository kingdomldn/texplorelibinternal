Image Modules

Image modules are the simplest experience in the app to both create and build. When the user scans the page they are presented with either a full screen image.

Even though the image module is simple in nature it can be extended. Further information can be added to the image so long as it is flattened into the image itself. An example could be highlights of the car pulled out. The full screen image experience can also for example display an animated gif, note that the quality of this experience can vary depending on the asset.
