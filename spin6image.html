6 Image Snapspin Spinner

Functionality overview

The 6 image snapspin module was custom designed and built for the Prius 2016 brochure. The module consists of a circular image rotating as the user swipes on the screen. When the user swipes the wheel spins to the next segment. This module features 6 content areas allowing for different content in each segment. The 6 spin features basic parralax like effects across three layers although these are limited due to the circular nature of the module.

The 6 image snapspin module has been used in the Prius brochure to display its ability to transition from different environments. The module itself has 6 segments on the module to utilise although the Prius model only uses 3 and repeats the others. With a small piece of simple text content the module can be used for various different scenarios. Although the module itself doesn't take very long to populate into the app itself the production time to produce the imagery required can take longer varying depending on complexity and effects.
